FAQs!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is VA Hacks?

VA Hacks is a 9-hour hackathon located at _____. Students form teams to come up with new and inVAtive technological solutions.

Who can attend VA Hacks?

Anyone who is attending middle or high school can compete.

Is there a registration fee?

No! VA Hacks is free thanks to our sponsors!

What should I bring?

You should bring a computer, phone, chargers, and extension cords. You may want to bring an extra pair of clothing (business casual attire) for your presentation. Feel free to bring anything else that you think may help your team.

Do I need a team?

Yes. You will need to form a team of two to four students. If you do not have a team, we will help you find a group to work with once you arrive at VA Hacks.

Will there be food?

Yes! We will provide lunch, a snack and dinner. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know through the Registration Form.

What if I don't have any prior experience or knowledge about coding?

Come to VA Hacks! No experience is needed. If you do not know how to code, attend the workshops and ask mentors for help!

Who will be judges for the event?

The judges will comprise of volunteers and individuals from our sponsors.
